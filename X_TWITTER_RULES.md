## X Rules (Content Policies)

### Safety Policies

**Violence:**
- Cannot threaten violence
- Cannot glorify violence
- Cannot share graphic violence without warning/context
- Terrorist and violent extremist content prohibited

**Child Safety (Zero Tolerance):**
- No CSAM (child sexual abuse material)
- No exploitation of minors
- No sexualization of minors
- Report immediately if encountered

**Abuse and Harassment:**
- Cannot target individuals with harassment
- Cannot incite others to harass
- Cannot share private information (doxxing)
- Cannot engage in coordinated harassment

**Hateful Conduct:**
- Cannot promote violence against groups
- Cannot dehumanize based on protected categories
- Includes: race, ethnicity, national origin, sexual orientation, gender, gender identity, religious affiliation, age, disability, serious disease

**Suicide and Self-Harm:**
- Cannot promote or encourage suicide/self-harm
- Cannot share detailed methods
- Can share recovery stories and support resources

### Authenticity Policies

**Platform Manipulation:**
- Cannot artificially amplify content
- Cannot use multiple accounts deceptively
- Cannot buy/sell accounts
- Cannot coordinate inauthentic activity
- Cannot use automation for harmful purposes

**Spam:**
- Cannot post duplicate content repeatedly
- Cannot aggressively follow/unfollow
- Cannot send unsolicited DMs at scale
- Cannot use deceptive links

**Impersonation:**
- Cannot impersonate individuals, groups, or organizations
- Cannot use misleading profile information
- Parody accounts must be clearly labeled

**Synthetic and Manipulated Media:**
- Cannot share deceptive synthetic media
- Labeled when detected as AI-generated
- Context may be added by Community Notes

### Civic Integrity

**Elections:**
- Cannot share false information about voting
- Cannot mislead about election processes
- Cannot intimidate voters

### Misleading Information

**Health Misinformation:**
- Restrictions on dangerous health claims
- Vaccine misinformation policies
- COVID-19 policies (check current status)

## Sponsored Content and Disclosures

### X's Requirements

**For paid partnerships:**
- Use clear disclosure
- #ad or #sponsored recommended
- Cannot be misleading about sponsorship

### FTC Requirements (Still Apply)

**Must disclose:**
- Paid sponsorships
- Free products received
- Affiliate relationships
- Any material connection

**Disclosure Format:**

Good:
```
#ad Loving this new [product] from [brand]! [content]
```

```
Partnering with @brand | [content]
```

```
Sponsored by @brand | [content]
```

Not sufficient:
```
Thanks @brand! [content]  // Unclear if paid
#partner  // Ambiguous
```

### Placement

- Disclosure should be early in post
- Not hidden in hashtag chains
- Visible without clicking "Show more"

## Content Best Practices

### What Works on X

- Concise, clear messaging
- Engagement with community
- Threads for longer content
- Original thoughts and perspectives
- Timely commentary

### What to Avoid

**Platform Manipulation:**
- Buying followers/engagement
- Coordinated engagement (pods)
- Excessive automation
- Repetitive posting

**Content Issues:**
- Excessive promotional content
- Spam-like behavior
- Misleading claims
- Aggressive selling

### Sensitive Media Settings

**If posting mature content:**
- Mark account as containing sensitive media
- Mark individual posts when appropriate
- Follow adult content policies if applicable

**Adult Content Policy:**
- Must be in designated adult accounts
- Cannot be promoted
- Limited discovery
- Age-gating applied

## Enforcement

### Account Actions

| Violation | Possible Action |
|-----------|-----------------|
| First minor violation | Warning |
| Repeated violations | Temporary suspension |
| Severe violations | Permanent suspension |
| Extreme violations | Immediate permanent ban |

### Visibility Filtering

X may limit visibility of content/accounts:
- Reduced reach
- Not appearing in search
- Not recommended
- Labeled with warnings

### Account Suspension

**Temporary:**
- Features restricted
- Must complete required actions
- Usually time-limited

**Permanent:**
- Account access removed
- Cannot create new accounts
- Limited appeal options

### Appeals

**How to Appeal:**
1. Follow link in suspension notice
2. Complete appeal form
3. Explain why suspension was incorrect
4. Wait for review

**Timeline:** Varies, can take days to weeks.

## Posting Guidelines for PRINTMAXX Content

### Thread Best Practices

```
1/ [Hook - main value proposition]

2/ [Supporting point 1]

3/ [Supporting point 2]

4/ [Example or proof]

5/ [Call to action]

If this was helpful, follow @[handle] for more.
```

### Promotional Content Balance

- Follow 80/20 rule: 80% value, 20% promotion
- Engage authentically with others
- Don't be overly salesy
- Provide genuine value

### Avoiding Spam Signals

- Don't post same content repeatedly
- Don't use excessive hashtags
- Don't aggressively follow/unfollow
- Don't send unsolicited promotional DMs
- Space out promotional posts
