## Content That's Prohibited

### Violence and Dangerous Organizations

**Not Allowed:**
- Graphic violence
- Content promoting violence
- Support for terrorist or criminal organizations
- Threats of violence
- Dangerous individuals' content

### Hate Speech

**Not Allowed:**
- Attacks based on protected characteristics:
  - Race, ethnicity, national origin
  - Religious affiliation
  - Sexual orientation
  - Gender identity
  - Disability
  - Disease
- Dehumanizing language
- Calls for exclusion or segregation

### Nudity and Sexual Content

**Not Allowed:**
- Sexual activity
- Genitals
- Close-ups of fully nude buttocks
- Female nipples (exceptions: breastfeeding, mastectomy, protests)
- Sexual solicitation

**May Be Restricted:**
- Revealing clothing
- Suggestive poses
- Focus on body parts

### Bullying and Harassment

**Not Allowed:**
- Repeated unwanted contact
- Targeted harassment
- Privacy violations
- Sharing intimate images without consent
- Threats to release private information

### Intellectual Property

**Not Allowed:**
- Copyright infringement
- Trademark violations
- Posting others' content without permission

**Note:** Instagram has a strike system for copyright claims.

### Fraud and Spam

**Not Allowed:**
- Misrepresentation
- Fake engagement (buying likes/followers)
- Spam comments or DMs
- Phishing attempts
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior

### False Information

**Not Allowed:**
- Health misinformation
- Election misinformation
- Manipulated media intended to deceive

**May Be Restricted:**
- Content rated false by fact-checkers gets reduced distribution

### Regulated Goods

**Restricted:**
- Alcohol (age-gated, with restrictions)
- Tobacco products (not allowed)
- Drugs and drug paraphernalia (not allowed)
- Weapons (significant restrictions)
- Pharmaceuticals (restrictions)

## Branded Content and Advertising

### Branded Content Tool

**When to Use:**
- Receiving payment for content
- Receiving free products for promotion
- Any commercial relationship

**How to Use:**
1. Create your post
2. Go to Advanced Settings
3. Enable "Add paid partnership label"
4. Tag the brand partner

**Result:** "Paid partnership with [Brand]" label appears

### Branded Content Requirements

**Must:**
- Use branded content tool for all paid partnerships
- Clearly disclose commercial relationships
- Only tag business partners you have relationships with
- Follow all advertising policies

**Must Not:**
- Hide sponsored nature of content
- Promote prohibited products
- Make false claims
- Tag brands without actual relationship

### Prohibited in Branded Content

Cannot promote:
- Tobacco and related products
- Weapons, ammunition, explosives
- Adult products/services
- Illegal products
- Drugs (including CBD in many cases)
- Unsafe supplements
- Discriminatory practices

### Partnership Ads (Formerly Branded Content Ads)

**Requirements:**
- Creator must grant ad permissions
- Brand gets access to promote as ad
- All advertising policies apply
- Must use branded content tag

## Monetization Eligibility

### Partner Monetization Policies

To access monetization features (badges, subscriptions, bonuses), you must meet:

**Community Standards:**
- Follow Community Guidelines
- No recent violations
- Account in good standing

**Content Monetization Policies:**
- Original content
- No recycled/reposted content
- No static images with audio
- No low-quality or artificial content
- No misinformation
- No controversial content

### Ineligible Content for Monetization

**Cannot monetize:**
- Debated social issues content
- Violence and graphic content
- Misinformation
- Regulated goods content
- Inappropriate content for brands
- Low-quality content

### Creator Eligibility

**General Requirements:**
- 18+ years old
- Follow Partner Monetization Policies
- Meet product-specific requirements
- Located in eligible country

**For Instagram Subscriptions:**
- 10,000+ followers
- Professional account
- 18+ years old

**For Badges (Live):**
- 10,000+ followers
- Professional account

## Content Best Practices

### Avoiding Restrictions

**Shadowbanning Risk Factors:**
- Using banned hashtags
- Engagement pod participation
- Rapid following/unfollowing
- Automated tools
- Repeated guideline violations
- Spam behavior

**Best Practices:**
- Use relevant hashtags (not banned ones)
- Engage authentically
- Post original content
- Vary your content types
- Respond to comments genuinely

### Hashtag Guidelines

**Avoid:**
- Banned hashtags (list changes frequently)
- Irrelevant hashtags
- Excessive hashtags in unnatural ways
- Hashtags that attract spam

**Check if hashtag is banned:**
- Search the hashtag
- If no recent posts show, likely restricted

### Reels Best Practices

**Encouraged:**
- Original content
- Full-screen vertical video
- Engaging content
- Trending audio (when appropriate)

**Discouraged:**
- Reposted TikTok content (with watermark)
- Low-resolution video
- Excessive text covering video
- Repurposed content from other platforms

## Shopping and Commerce

### Instagram Shopping Requirements

**Eligibility:**
- Business or Creator account
- Comply with commerce policies
- Sell physical goods (primarily)
- Have a website with checkout
- Be in supported market

**Prohibited Products:**
- Weapons
- Tobacco
- Adult products
- Drugs
- Live animals
- Healthcare products (restrictions)
- Recalled products

### Product Tagging

**When tagging products:**
- Only tag products you're actually featuring
- Products must be in the image/video
- Follow commerce policies
- Disclose if affiliate

## Affiliate Marketing on Instagram

### Disclosure Requirements

**FTC Compliance:**
- Clear disclosure of affiliate relationship
- Disclosure before the link
- Use: #ad, #affiliate, "affiliate link"
- Cannot rely solely on "Paid Partnership" label

**Instagram Affiliate Features:**
- Native affiliate tagging (for eligible creators)
- Automatically discloses "Eligible for commission"

### Best Practices

```
Caption Example:

I've been loving this [product]! Here's my honest review.

[Content about product]

Shop it here: [link in bio]

#ad #affiliate - I may earn a small commission if you purchase
```

## Enforcement and Appeals

### Violation Consequences

| Severity | Consequence |
|----------|-------------|
| Minor first offense | Content removed, warning |
| Repeated violations | Feature restrictions |
| Continued violations | Account suspension |
| Severe violations | Immediate account removal |

### What Gets Your Content Removed

- Community Guidelines violations
- Copyright claims
- Report from another user (if valid)
- Automated detection systems

### Appealing Decisions

**For Content Removal:**
1. Go to Account Status in Settings
2. View removed content
3. Select "Request Review"
4. Wait for decision (usually 24 hours)

**For Account Disabling:**
1. Follow link in email notification
2. Submit appeal form
3. May need to verify identity
4. Longer review time
