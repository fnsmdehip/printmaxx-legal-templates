# Gumroad Seller Compliance Guide

**Policies for Selling Digital Products**

**IMPORTANT:** Gumroad policies can change. Always verify current rules at help.gumroad.com. This is a summary for reference, not a substitute for reading the official terms.

## Overview

Gumroad is a popular platform for selling digital products, memberships, and courses. Understanding their policies helps you sell successfully while avoiding account issues.

**Key Resources:**
- Terms of Service: gumroad.com/terms
- Seller Guidelines: help.gumroad.com
- Prohibited Items: In Terms of Service

## What You Can Sell on Gumroad

### Allowed Products

**Digital Products:**
- Ebooks and PDFs
- Online courses
- Templates and worksheets
- Software and apps
- Music and audio
- Art and graphics
- Fonts
- Videos
- Memberships/subscriptions
- Pre-orders

**Physical Products:**
- Now supported
- Must handle fulfillment yourself
- Shipping costs separate

**Services:**
- Coaching sessions
- Consulting calls
- Custom work (with delivery mechanism)

### Partially Restricted

**Adult Content:**
- Allowed with restrictions
- Must be marked as NSFW
- Cannot be shown on Discover
- Age verification implications
- Payment processor restrictions may apply

**Affiliate Products:**
- Can sell with proper rights
- Cannot resell others' work without permission
- PLR (Private Label Rights) products allowed if license permits

## What You Cannot Sell

### Prohibited Items

**Absolutely Prohibited:**
- Illegal content
- Child exploitation material (zero tolerance)
- Non-consensual intimate images
- Stolen or pirated content
- Counterfeit goods
- Weapons
- Drugs and drug paraphernalia
- Malware or harmful software
- Personal data of others
- Services that violate laws

**Restricted Categories:**
- Hate speech or discriminatory content
- Content promoting violence
- Gambling products
- Multi-level marketing (MLM) schemes
- Get-rich-quick schemes with false promises
- Pyramid schemes
- Products that infringe intellectual property

### Content Restrictions

**Cannot Include:**
- Copyrighted material without rights
- Trademarks used without permission
- Others' personal information
- Defamatory content
- Content that violates third-party rights

## Seller Account Requirements

### Account Setup

**Required:**
- Valid email address
- Payment method (Stripe or PayPal)
- Tax information (W-9 for US, W-8BEN for international)
- Accurate account information
- Age 18+

### Profile Requirements

**Should Include:**
- Accurate name or business name
- Valid contact information
- Clear product descriptions
- Accurate pricing

### Tax Compliance

**Gumroad Handles:**
- Collecting VAT/GST on digital products in supported regions
- Remitting taxes in applicable jurisdictions
- Providing receipts with tax information

**You Handle:**
- Reporting income to tax authorities
- Paying income taxes
- Maintaining records

**Stripe Express/PayPal:**
- You receive payouts directly
- 1099-K issued if threshold met (US)
- Responsible for your own tax reporting

## Pricing and Payments

### Pricing Guidelines

**Allowed:**
- Set your own prices
- "Pay what you want" pricing
- Minimum price with "pay what you want"
- Free products
- Discounts and sales
- Tiered pricing

**Best Practices:**
- Price reflects value
- No deceptive pricing
- Clear about what's included

### Payment Processing

**Gumroad Fees (as of last check):**
- Check current fee structure at gumroad.com/features
- Fees vary by plan (free vs. Premium)
- Additional payment processing fees

**Payouts:**
- To your connected Stripe/PayPal
- Payout schedule varies
- Minimum payout threshold may apply

### Refund Policy

**Gumroad's Position:**
- Sellers set their own refund policies
- Must honor stated policy
- Disputes handled per Gumroad's terms

**Recommended Approach:**
- Clear refund policy on product page
- Reasonable timeframe (14-30 days common)
- Process refunds promptly
- Document policy clearly

## Product Listings

### Required Information

**Product Page Should Include:**
- Clear product title
- Accurate description of what's included
- Price
- File format/delivery method
- Any system requirements
- Refund policy

### Images and Media

**Requirements:**

**Recommendations:**

## Intellectual Property

### Your Responsibilities

**You Must:**
- Own or have rights to sell your content
- Not infringe copyrights, trademarks, or patents
- Respect others' intellectual property
- Respond to valid takedown requests

### Copyright Claims

**If You Receive a Claim:**
1. Gumroad may suspend product
2. Review the claim
3. File counter-notice if you believe claim is invalid
4. Repeated claims may result in account termination

**If Your Work Is Stolen:**
1. File DMCA takedown with Gumroad
2. Provide required information
3. Gumroad reviews and acts on valid claims

## Marketing and Promotion

### On Gumroad

**Discover:**
- Your products may appear in Gumroad Discover
- Adult content excluded
- Quality/sales metrics affect visibility

**Affiliate Program:**
- Enable Gumroad's affiliate feature
- Set commission rates
- Affiliates promote your products

### Off Gumroad

**Allowed:**
- Promote on social media
- Email marketing
- Paid advertising
- Content marketing
- Affiliate marketing

**Requirements:**
- Honest claims about products
- No false advertising
- Proper disclosures for affiliates
- Comply with platform advertising policies

### Income Claims

**If Your Product Promises Income:**
- Must be honest about results
- Cannot guarantee specific income
- Include appropriate disclaimers
- Risk of extra scrutiny

**Disclaimer Template:**
```
Results vary. This product provides information/tools, not guaranteed
outcomes. Your results depend on many factors including your effort,
skills, and market conditions. We make no income guarantees.
```

## Account Standing

### Maintaining Good Standing

**Do:**
- Follow Terms of Service
- Respond to Gumroad communications
- Update payment/tax information
- Provide good customer service
- Keep products compliant

**Don't:**
- Sell prohibited items
- Misrepresent products
- Ignore customer complaints
- Accumulate excessive chargebacks
- Violate intellectual property

**Warning Signs:**
- Excessive refund requests
- Multiple copyright claims
- Chargeback rate too high
- Customer complaints
- Policy violations

**Potential Consequences:**
- Product suspension
- Feature restrictions
- Account suspension
- Permanent ban
- Funds held

### Appeals

**If Account Suspended:**
1. Check email for explanation
2. Contact Gumroad support
3. Provide requested information
4. Address underlying issues
5. Request review
