## Content That's Prohibited

### Dangerous Acts and Challenges

**Not Allowed:**
- Content showing dangerous activities that could lead to injury
- Dangerous challenges or dares
- Misuse of dangerous tools or weapons
- Content that could encourage harmful behavior

**Examples:**
- Fire challenges
- Choking games
- Consuming dangerous substances
- Dangerous stunts without safety measures

### Violence and Graphic Content

**Not Allowed:**
- Graphic violence, gore, or disturbing imagery
- Content promoting violence against individuals or groups
- Threats of violence
- Content showing animal cruelty
- Content depicting or promoting self-harm

### Hateful Behavior

**Not Allowed:**
- Content attacking people based on protected attributes:
  - Race, ethnicity, national origin
  - Religion
  - Caste
  - Sexual orientation
  - Gender identity
  - Immigration status
  - Disability
  - Serious disease
- Slurs and derogatory terms
- Content promoting hateful ideologies

### Harassment and Bullying

**Not Allowed:**
- Targeting individuals with harassment
- Encouraging others to harass
- Revealing personal information (doxxing)
- Degrading or shaming individuals
- Sexual harassment

### Nudity and Sexual Content

**Not Allowed:**
- Nudity (exposed genitals, female nipples)
- Sexually explicit content
- Sexual solicitation
- Content sexualizing minors (zero tolerance)

**Restricted:**
- Suggestive content may have limited distribution
- Revealing clothing may trigger age restrictions

### Minor Safety

**Zero Tolerance for:**
- Content sexualizing minors
- Content depicting abuse of minors
- Grooming behaviors
- Content encouraging dangerous activities by minors

**Requirements:**
- Users must be 13+ to use TikTok
- Users must be 18+ for certain features (live, gifts)

### Integrity and Authenticity

**Not Allowed:**
- Impersonation of others
- Misleading information about identity
- Coordinated inauthentic behavior
- Spam and fake engagement
- Manipulation of platform features

**Required:**
- Genuine accounts representing real people/entities
- Accurate profile information

### Misinformation

**Not Allowed:**
- Harmful misinformation about:
  - Health and medical topics
  - Elections and civic processes
  - Climate change denial
  - Dangerous conspiracy theories
- Manipulated media (deepfakes) intended to deceive

### Illegal Activities

**Not Allowed:**
- Content promoting illegal activities
- Sale of illegal goods
- Drug-related content
- Weapons trafficking
- Fraud and scams

## Advertising and Branded Content

### Branded Content Requirements

**Must:**
- Use the branded content toggle when you have a commercial relationship
- Clearly disclose sponsored content
- Follow FTC guidelines for disclosures
- Not promote prohibited products

**Branded Content Toggle:**
- Required when receiving compensation
- Required when receiving free products for promotion
- Shows "Paid Partnership" label

### Prohibited Advertising Categories

**Cannot Promote:**
- Tobacco and vaping products
- Illegal drugs
- Weapons and ammunition
- Adult products and services
- Gambling (with exceptions for legal operators in some regions)
- Counterfeit goods
- Financial products (restrictions apply)
- Weight loss products (significant restrictions)
- Cryptocurrency (significant restrictions)

### Spark Ads (Whitelisting)

**Requirements:**
- Creator must authorize via TikTok's authorization system
- Content must comply with advertising policies
- Disclosure requirements apply
- Cannot promote prohibited categories

## Creator Monetization Requirements

### TikTok Creator Fund / Creativity Program

**Eligibility:**
- 18+ years old
- 10,000+ followers (requirements vary by program)
- 100,000+ video views in last 30 days
- Account in good standing
- Based in eligible country
- Original content only

**Content Requirements:**
- Videos must be original
- Must comply with community guidelines
- Must comply with Terms of Service
- Certain content types may not be eligible

### LIVE Gifts

**Eligibility:**
- 18+ years old
- 1,000+ followers
- Account in good standing

**Rules:**
- Cannot solicit gifts through manipulation
- Cannot exchange gifts for prohibited activities
- Must follow community guidelines during LIVE

### TikTok Shop

**Seller Requirements:**
- Business verification
- Compliance with seller policies
- Product safety requirements
- Return and refund policies

**Prohibited Products:**
- Many categories (alcohol, tobacco, weapons, etc.)
- Check TikTok Shop prohibited items list

## Content Best Practices for Compliance

### Staying Safe

**Do:**
- Review community guidelines regularly
- Use content warnings when appropriate
- Be mindful of trending sounds/challenges (some are banned)
- Monitor comments for violations
- Respond to guideline notifications promptly

**Don't:**
- Assume because others do it, it's allowed
- Ignore warning notices
- Try to circumvent content filters
- Use banned hashtags or sounds
- Repeatedly post borderline content

### Age-Restricted Content

Some content may be allowed but age-restricted:
- Violence in news/educational context
- Mature themes
- Content not suitable for younger audiences

Age-restricted content has limited distribution.

### Hashtag Cautions

**Be Careful With:**
- Trending hashtags (may become banned)
- Challenge hashtags (some lead to violations)
- Political hashtags (may trigger restrictions)
- Health-related hashtags (strict scrutiny)

## Account Enforcement

### Warning System

| Action | Consequence |
|--------|-------------|
| First violation | Warning, content removed |
| Repeated violations | Feature restrictions |
| Continued violations | Account ban (temporary) |
| Severe violations | Permanent ban |

### What Happens When Content Is Removed

1. You receive a notification
2. Content is no longer visible
3. You can appeal within the app
4. Appeals are reviewed by TikTok team

### Appealing Violations

**Process:**
1. Go to notification
2. Select "Appeal"
3. Provide context/explanation
4. Wait for review (usually 24-48 hours)

**Tips:**
- Be polite and factual
- Explain any misunderstanding
- Provide context that may not be obvious
- Only appeal if you believe decision was incorrect

### Permanent Bans

**May result from:**
- Severe first-time violations (CSAM, terrorism)
- Repeated violations despite warnings
- Circumventing previous bans
- Organized harmful behavior

**Limited appeal options for permanent bans.**

## Business and Creator Account Features

### Business Account vs Creator Account

| Feature | Personal | Creator | Business |
|---------|----------|---------|----------|
| Analytics | Limited | Yes | Yes |
| Commercial music | No | Limited | Yes |
| Promote feature | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Contact buttons | No | Yes | Yes |
| Shopping features | No | Yes | Yes |

### Commercial Music

**Business accounts:**
- Can only use music from Commercial Music Library
- Cannot use popular songs in ads
- Limited organic sound options

**Creator accounts:**
- Can use full music library for organic content
- Restrictions apply for branded content
- Some music not available for ads
