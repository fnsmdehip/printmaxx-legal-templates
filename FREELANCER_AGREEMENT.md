## 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 3.1 Ownership of Deliverables

**Option A - Full Assignment (recommended for UGC):**

- Copyright in all content created
- Right to modify, adapt, and create derivative works
- Right to use in any media, platform, or format
- Right to use in perpetuity, worldwide

Freelancer waives any moral rights to the extent permitted by law.

**Option B - Limited License:**

Freelancer grants Client a [exclusive/non-exclusive], [perpetual/X-year], worldwide license to use the Deliverables for:
- [SPECIFY PERMITTED USES]

Freelancer retains underlying copyright and may use for portfolio purposes only.

### 3.2 Work Made for Hire

To the extent permitted by law, Deliverables are considered "works made for hire" as defined under U.S. copyright law. If any Deliverable is not a work made for hire, Freelancer assigns all rights as described in Section 3.1.
